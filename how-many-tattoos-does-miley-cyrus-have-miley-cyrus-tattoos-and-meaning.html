How many tattoos does Miley Cyrus have?

Miley Cyrus has over 74 tattoos on her body and all of them have their meaning. The tattoos include “We Babies”, Motorcycle”, Freedom, “I’m proud of u” and many others. Miley is one of the most popular musicians in the world who has a lot of several tattoos on their body. Keep reading as we give you all the meanings of Miley Cyrus’s Tattoos.

Miley Cyrus Tattoos and Meaning

• “I’m proud of u”

“I’m proud of u” is one of the written tattoos on the pop singer’s body. This tattoo was a hand-written note from Yoko Ono to Miley Cyrus. The delicate lettering reads: “I’m proud of u, Yoko.”

• Motorcycle

She did this tattoo to pay a tribute to her father, Billy Ray Cyrus. The motorcycle is a replica of her dad Billy Ray Cyrus. The motorcycle tattoo is located on her shoulder and it was drawn by Dr Woo.

• “We Babies”

She got the “We Babies” together with Pete Davidson. They decided to get the tats after Miley and Pete appeared in a skit together as “rapping babies.” Pete has removed the tattoo but Miley still has the tattoo on her left ankle.

• “freedom”

Miley did this tattoo after her breakup with her husband Liam Hemsworth. The freedom tattoo is located at her knuckle, which signifies a nod to her single “Mother’s Daughter.”

• “Rose”

Rose is one of the tattoos she had in October 2021. Miley has not given any reason behind this tattoo.

• “Ancient Symbols”

The “ancient symbols found on rock walls” tattoo is found on her arm. Miley was inspired to get this tattoo through her experience with breakups. The tattoo was done by Dr Woo.

• Rock ‘n’ Roll Heart

Miley and her new lover Cody Simpson got these tats together. Miley’s tattoo is a heart with an arrow that reads “rock ‘n’ roll heart,” while Cody got a skull with a sickle.

• Crossed arrows

The cross arrows are found on her right elbow. The cross arrows symbol is an American symbol which means friendship. The tattoo was done by Kat Von D.

• Roman numerals, “I’m in your corner,” & an anatomical heart

Miley has a VIIXCI” on the inside of her left elbow. Below the numbers, she has a drawing of an anatomical heart by Leonardo da Vinci done by Kat Von D.
On her right bicep, Miley has a tattoo to honour her father, Billy Ray, and singer Johnny Cash. In 2017, she revealed in a Billboard cover interview, she said, “I’ve got a tattoo of Johnny Cash’s autograph that he gave me when I was a ¬little girl that says, ‘I’m in your corner.'”

• A claw Tattoo

Miley has a curved claw symbol on her right bicep, but she has not revealed the meaning of the tattoo yet. The tattoo was inked by a celebrity artist, Kat Von D.

• Mammie Tattoo

This tattoo was done to pay a tribute to her grandmother in 2013. Miley posted a pic of the tattoo on Twitter and wrote, “Because I am her favourite & she is mine.” So sweet!

• The Vegemite Tattoo

The Vegemite tattoo is on her left arm. This tattoo was done to pay a tribute to her ex-husband Liam. He’s a huge fan of the Australian spread — he also has a similar design on his ankle.

• The avocado Tattoo

The avocado Tattoo is found on her left arm. She got this tattoo in July 2015 from her friend Lauren Winzer.

  • “KATZ” tattoo

It’s no secret this The Voice judge loves a tribute tattoo. The word, “KATZ” on the back of her arm is for her BFF Katy Weaver. Katy also has a matching one, “MEOWTZ” on her right wrist.

  • “Just Breathe” on her rib cage

Miley did the “Just Breathe” tattoo to honour her grandfather and best friend when she was 17 years old.

  • Heart tattoo on her left arm

She also has a heart with the initials “WA” in it. She has not revealed the meaning of this tattoo yet but many believe it’s a tattoo to honour Woody Allen.

  • The “Love” Tattoo

The “Love” tattoo is found on her right ear. She got this tattoo in 2010 and the reason behind it is to “shut down all the crap that everyone throws into your ears.” She said to Access Hollywood, “[You’re only supposed to hear the things coming from the people that genuinely love you.”

Miley has at least seven tattoos on her right hand. These include a heart, evil eye, alien head, watermelon slice, peace sign, cross, and the words “BAD” in red as a tribute to popular singer, Michael Jackson.

  • A dream catcher on her right side

Miley did the dream catcher tattoo in 2011 and it symbolizes protection for her four brothers and sisters.

  • A tribute to Floyd (her dog) on her left side

She got a dog tattoo on her left side in 2014. Miley did the tattoo to remember her late dog named Floyd. The tattoo is a drawing of Floyd and the words, “With a little help from my friends.” We’re not crying, you’re crying!

  • An anchor

The anchor tattoo is found on her right wrist. Miley defined this symbol as safety and a reminder to stay grounded. The tat was done by Fabio Satori while on tour in Brazil.

  • “Om,” and a cat on her left arm

Miley also has the “Om” symbol, a sacred Hindu reference, tatted on her wrist as well as a cat head on her left elbow.

  • “Wükong”

Miley did the “Wükong” tattoo to honour, her junior brother Braison. The name Wükong is her nickname for Braison.

  • “Love Never Dies”

She also has the words “love never dies” on her left side.

  • A naked woman on her left side

She also has a tattoo of a naked woman holding a tiny baby. Miley did this inked this tattoo in 2015.

  • A smiley face

The smiley face tattoo is found on her left ankle. The tattoo was done by Cheyne Thomas in July 2014.

  • A skull Tattoo

The skull tattoo is found on her right ankle. She did this tattoo with her ex-partner Liam Hemsworth.

  • A moon, fish, and a tribute to Emu (her dog)

Miley has several tattoos on his left hand including a crescent moon and tributes to her pets, Pablow (her deceased fish) and Emu (her sheepdog). The tattoo of her dog Emu was done by Doctor Woo.

  • Theodore Roosevelt quote on

In 2012, Miley tattooed her left forearm with Theodore Roosevelt quote, “so that his place shall never be with those cold and timid souls who neither know victory nor defeat.”
