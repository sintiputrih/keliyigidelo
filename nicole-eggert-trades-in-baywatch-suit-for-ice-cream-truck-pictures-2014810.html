Nicole Eggert Trades In Baywatch Suit For Ice Cream Truck: Pictures

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  252 words  |  Martina Birk

Nicole Eggert has traded in her famous red bathing suit for some cherry popsicles! The Baywatch star recently revealed via Twitter that she’s launched a new career as an ice cream truck driver.

Eggert, 42, gushed that she’s now the proud owner of a mint green ice cream truck on Sunday, Sept. 21. “My new truck @scoops_truck thank u @darksidemotor!!!! I absolutely love it!” she wrote.

My baby girl! Done beautifully by @darkside_motoring @scoopsicecreamtruck www.scoopsicecreamtruck.com #scoopsicecreamtruck

View on Instagram

The actress’ new company Scoops already has its own website and is available for private events and parties. Labeled as an “Ice Cream Shoppe on Wheels,” the truck offers scooped ice cream cones, individual sized ice cream cakes, and a variety of candies.

Come get a scoop from my truck tomorrow on Radford and Ventura in Studio City!!! #scoopsicecreamtruck #goodtimes #weallscreamforicecream

The Charles in Charge alum has even been giving followers the exact location to find her on a daily basis. “Come get a scoop from my truck tomorrow on Radford and Ventura in Studio City!!!” she captioned an Instagram picture of three colorful ice cream cones with sprinkles on Sept. 21.
