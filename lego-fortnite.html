How To Find Malachite In LEGO Fortnite

 Posted on February 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  321 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Here's what you need to do to find Malachite in LEGO Fortnite.

First Published December 22, 2023, 17:00

LEGO Fortnite is all about survival and for that to happen, players will have to build a lot of different objects and tools. The tools that players create will give them food, weapons, and even help make their own transportation system.

The key to making all of these items are materials, like Brightcore and Malachite which can only be found in certain areas and can only be gathered in a certain way. Here, we will go over how to get Malachite in LEGO Fortnite.

How To Find Malachite In LEGO Fortnite

The main trouble with materials in LEGO Fortnite is finding them. Malachite is a pretty high-level material and it can be difficult to find if players don't know where to start. Malachite can be found in the Frostlands otherwise known as the snowy biome. So bringing some items to keep players warm is essential.

But Malachite cannot be found just anywhere in the Frostlands. Players will have to scale mountains made out of ice to find this material. To climb up the ice mountains, creating a wooden staircase will be essential. For reference, Malachite is a bright green-looking rock that is sticking out of the ground.

How To Collect Malachite In LEGO Fortnite

Malachite is a very high-level item and it is not easy to farm. Players will have to create an Epic Pickaxe to farm the Malachite. Anything less will not work. This means that players will also need a Level 4 Crafting Bench to make the Epic Pickaxe. Don't go hunting for Malachite without this tool.

To make an Epic Pickaxe, players will need 8 Obsidian Slabs and 5 Frostpine Rods. On top of the Epic Pickaxe, any protection against the cold will be useful. Spicy Burgers are the easiest way to protect against the cold, as well as Spicy Peppers.
