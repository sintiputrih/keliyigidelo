About

Rapper best known for his song "I Don't Eat Ass." The YouTube video for that song has garnered over 500,000 views. He also has a popular Instagram account that operates under the name Nutstar. It has garnered 170,000 followers. His videos feature tongue-in-cheek comedy along with his signature deadpan delivery.

In late 2013, he began posting on Instagram. His first post was a photo of him smiling with the caption, "Wrigley Field."

He was born in Phoenix, Arizona.

In June of 2023, he posted a selfie that featured himself alongside Cody Ko. He has featured music by Marvin Gaye in his TikTok videos.
