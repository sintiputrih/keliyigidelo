Josh Peck Reacts After Former Co-Star Drake Bell Pleads Guilty to Child Endangerment Charges

 Posted on March 8, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  164 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Josh Peck is sharing his thoughts on the sentencing of his former Drake & Josh co-star Drake Bell.

The two actors got their start on the Nickelodeon series The Amanda Show before getting their own series in 2004.

Drake was charged with attempted endangering of children and disseminating matter harmful to juveniles in Ohio. Earlier this week, he plead guilty and was sentenced to two years’ probation and 200 hours of community service.

The victim in the child endangerment case gave a statement during the case in which she revealed many upsetting details about what allegedly happened between them.

While appearing at the premiere of his Disney+ series Turner & Hooch, Josh spoke to Variety and gave a quick reaction to the charges.

“It’s upsetting, and it’s an unfortunate situation. It’s disappointing,” he said.
