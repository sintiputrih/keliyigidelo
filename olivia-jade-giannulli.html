Olivia Jade Giannulli (born September 28, 1999) is an American Youtuber, Beauty Blogger, and Social Media Personality from Los Angeles, California. Furthermore, the 19-year-old Youtuber uploads videos on subjects like fashion, lifestyle, makeup, hair, Vlogs, viral games challenges etc.

The star made her first TV show appearance in the series “Entertainment Tonight” in 2015. Moreover, Giannulli is also extremely popular on Instagram having 1.3+ million followers. She is an internet sensation who updates her fans on a regular basis.

In addition, her most popular video on YouTube is “Teaching Parents Slang Terms“. The video also featured John Stamos (American actor, comedian, and singer) and David Dobrik (YouTube Star).

College Cheating Scandal & Controversy

Recently, Jade’s parents arrested with the college entrance exam cheating scandal. On March 12, 2019, the FBI and U.S. Attorney’s Office accused them of fraud and conspiracy charges.

Moreover, Lori took into custody along with 32 other rich parents, including Felicity Huffman (Desperate Housewives star). The indictment alleged that Lori and her spouse paid $500,000 USD to the Key Worldwide Foundation as a donation. But in reality, it was a bribe given to the University of Southern California’s admissions committee.

They wanted that their two daughters would join in the school’s women’s rowing team if they admitted. However, Olivia Jade and her sister never trained in the sport. This fraud is famous by the codename “Varsity Blues“.
