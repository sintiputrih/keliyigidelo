Idina Menzel is an American actress and singer whose career began on stage and later on Broadway. She played Maureen Johnson on the Broadway musical Rent. She was nominated for a Tony Award for this. She got her first Tony Award for Best Actress for her performance in Wicked. She ventured into film in the 2000s, reprising her role as Maureen Johnson in film version of Rent. She next starred as Nancy in the musical film Enchanted. She went on to become known on TV, thanks to her role as Shelby Corcoran on Glee. She voiced Queen Elsa in Frozen and sang “Let It Go”.
