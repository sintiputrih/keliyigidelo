Elena Samodanova Split With Husband Gleb Savchenko After 14 Years Of Marriage

 Posted on September 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  436 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Elena Samodanova and her husband of fourteen years, Gleb Savchenko, are no more together. The former lovebirds announced the decision of parting their ways through their respective social media platforms on 6 November 2020. Samodanova shared a picture on her Instagram story posing next to her estranged husband and added a custom text 'Breakup.'

Similarly, she wrote, 'After 14 years of marriage with my deepest sadness our road is coming to the end.'
