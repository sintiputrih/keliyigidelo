Meet Amber Beckford, star of Love Island 2022.

Love Island 2022 is back with a new lineup of young and sexy singletons ready to make their way to the Majorcan villa while keeping their viewers stuck to their TV set, while the contestants are having fun. The show is premiering on ITV2 on the evening of 6 June 2022. The line-up has many interesting contestants this year.

For the first time ever, viewers also play a significant role besides watching the show for fun. Now, they can vote on which contestant to move forward to the next round and who they think should couple up as a new Love Island twist in season 8.

Amber Beckford is the tenth contestant confirmed in the new line-up for season 8. Joining her on this interesting journey are model and dancer Tasha Ghouri, a student Liam Llewellyn from South Wales, Waitress and model Indiyah Polack, Fishmonger Luca Bish, Real Estate Agent Andrew Le Page, Pharmaceutical Sales Ikenna Ekwonna, business owner Gemma Owen, another business owner from Rome Italy Davide Sanclimenti, Paramedic Paige Thorne, and Senior Microbiologist Dami Hope are among the confirmed casts.

Whereas, Joshua Legrove, Jordan Spencer, Kat Harrison, Brad McDermott, Sophie Draper, and Ekin-Su Cülcüloğlu are rumored contestants on the show.

From hereon you’ll learn about her parents, age, birthday, height, and job.

Amber Beckford On Love Island 2022

Love Island’s Twitter page announced, “Amber is bringing main character energy to the villa, but will she find a man to match her vibes?”

Amber Beckford spoke with RadioTimes about what drove her to join the show she explained, “This year it definitely feels like it’s two feet in. I feel like now I’m most definitely ready, and wanting to look for a boyfriend”. About what she was bringing to the Villa she explained, “Hopefully fun! I can definitely be opinionated so I’m not sure how that’s going to go down.”

She is also interested to bond with the girls, adding: “With regards to Love Island, it is a show to find love. Girl code is definitely important to me, but I think it’s that balance isn’t it – I’ve got to put myself first!”

About the qualities she was looking for in the guy, she explained, “I don’t like guys that show off, like flashy, showy-offy guys – just have a personality and make me laugh”. She added about one date that was a complete disaster, “One guy tried to purposefully show off his car keys. We were in the middle of a restaurant”.

Plus, it is pride month, Amber is also representing the LGBTQ community and Tasha Ghouri after they shared a kiss in the first episode. In the show, she was also challenged to deliver her best chat-up to the girl she fancied the most. A male contestant Luca tried to shut her down when he quipped, “You can’t do that!”.

‘Why can’t I?’ Amber replied, before adding: ‘I’ve got a good one. Tash, fancy giving us a tash?’

This got the LGBTQ community riled up on Twitter. Some were excited and others were disappointed.

A week before the premiere of the show Amber announced, “It’s official and the secret is finally out! Our beautiful girl Amber will be joining ITV2’s Love Island this summer. Can’t wait to watch you on this journey! – Please support, show love and be kind”.

Who Is Amber Beckford Dating Today?

On 5 June 2022, Amber Beckford posted on her Instagram, “Amber & Dami, guys what are we thinking ??? 👀👀👀 #loveisland“. Fans also shared their thoughts in the comment section. One wrote, “Stunning couple!”. Another added, “I love them together 😍 but don’t think they are feeling it”.

A Love Island fan page commented, “I’m getting really good vibes from both of them!!”. Likewise, four commenters chimed in, “I quite like them together. Both are funny & could have a slow burn type romance.”

So, do they wound up together? We are yet to see that.

Before joining the show, Amber Beckford was in a serious relationship. She added, “After a serious relationship, I think I went through a little stage of just dating around and I think now I’m looking for a bit more of something serious”.

Although the show is appealing for fame and glitz, she prefers meeting people in person. She said: “I’m definitely a connection and spark kind of girl–I like to check the vibes first”. She added, “In bars or I’m out with friends and someone asks for your number–I definitely prefer that first encounter because I’m so awful on my phone. Just take me out, let’s go out and see and go from there!”.

Meanwhile, her Facebook relationship status states that she is single.

Amber Beckford Age

At the time of filming the show, Amber Beckford is 24 years old.

Amber Beckford Parents

Amber Beckford told Evening Standard her family was “fun, outspoken, crazy,” and a “wild child”. She is one of the four children born to her mother Lee Beckford.

Her sister Haylie works as a personal assistant at Ofcom. Haylie started her job in November 2021. She had also worked as a personal assistant & office manager at Morgan Capital, Senior Legal Assistant at MTA Solicitors LLP, Sales Assistant at TK Maxx, and a crew member at McDonald’s Corporation.

Haylie completed her education at LeSoCo College in Travel & Tourism in 2014. She attended Bromley College L2 Diploma in Business Administration & Law in 2015.

Amber also has another sister and a younger brother. Other people related to Amber are Hannah Williams-Beckford, Sue Beckford, Hollie Beckford, Jonah Beckford, and Kelly Beckford.
