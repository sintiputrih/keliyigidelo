'The Bachelorette' 2021 - 30 Contestants Revealed for Katie's Season

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  453 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Katie Thurston‘s season of The Bachelorette premieres TONIGHT (June 7) on ABC and we have all the info that you need on the 30 contestants.

The network only announced 29 of the 30 contestants and the last one was billed as a “mystery box,” but it seems we already know who that man will be.

Katie will be mentored by former Bachelorette stars Kaitlyn Bristowe and Tayshia Adams during the first evening and then she’ll pick 23 lucky bachelors to join her on the journey.

You might remember Katie from the 25th season of The Bachelor, in which she repeatedly stood up against bullying and negativity in the house while vying for Matt James‘ heart.

The Bachelorette‘s 17th season airs on Monday nights at 8/7c.
