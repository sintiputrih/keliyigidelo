Meiyang Chang is an Indian actor, television host, reality television personality, dancer, singer, and dentist. He has been a part of a variety of shows such as Indian Idol 3, Indian Idol 4, Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 4, India’s Got Talent 3, The Chair, Yeh Hai Aashiqui 3, Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi 6, Pyaar Tune Kya Kiya 1-3, Swiss Made Adventure, I Can Do That, Love Me India, Rising Star, Lost Essence of India, Epic IQ Challenge, Man’s World, 1962 – The War in the Hills, and Bombers. Moreover, Chang had also been cast in movies like Badmaash Company, Sultan, Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!, and Bharat.

    Meiyang Chang Facts

  • He is a 3rd generation Indian-Chinese.
  • Meiyang Chang made his first theatrical film appearance as Zing in the 2010 Hindi-language crime comedy film Badmaash Company. He co-starred in the film alongside Shahid Kapoor, Anushka Sharma, and Vir Das.
  • He appeared as Kanai Dao in the 2015 Hindi-language mystery action thriller film Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!, based on the fictional detective Byomkesh Bakshi created by the Bengali writer Sharadindu Bandyopadhyay.
  • In 2015, Meiyang Chang participated in the 6th season of the stunt-based reality competition show Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi and wrapped up his journey as a runner-up.
  • He brought to life the character of Jimmy Shergill in the 2019 Hindi-language drama film Bharat, starring Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif, Disha Patani, Sunil Grover, and Jackie Shroff.
