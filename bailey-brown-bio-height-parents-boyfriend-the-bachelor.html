Meet Bailey Brown, one of the contestants of The Bachelor season 27. While her journey unfolds on TV, here we discuss her family members. Here, you’ll discover about her parents, siblings, her career, boyfriend, and more.

Read all about her in this article below.

Bailey Brown On The Bachelor

Bailey Brown is one of the 30 women battling to win the heart of Zach Shallcross in season 27. If you need a reminder, Zach eliminated himself when his dream suite date with Season 19 Bachelorette Rachel Recchia didn’t go as expected, which caused, to put it mildly, some confusion. The identities of the 30 ladies vying for Zach’s affection this season have been released by ABC, and he’s ready to move on.

FWIW, it appears that there is a lot of competition.

Sadly, she is not Zach’s final four girls. According to Reality Steve, the final four contestants Zach chose are Gabi Elnicki, Ariel Frenkel, Charity Lawson, and Kaity Biggar.

More about Bailey, she is the best friend of season 20 Bachelor Ben Higgins’ wife, Jessica Clarke. Bailey and Zach met at The Bachelorette season 19 finale in September 2022, and Ben announced this in an episode of his podcast “Almost Famous.” The other four contestants who met Zach were Cat Wong, Brooklyn Willie, Christina Mandrell, and Brianna Thorbourne. Bailey was also one of the five contestants.

According to StyleCaster, Bailey was eliminated in 3rd rose ceremony along with Brianna Thorbourne and Christina Mandrell.

According to the spoiler Instagram account @BachelorWhatever, Gabriella “Gabi” Elnicki, who won The Bachelor season 27 and is engaged to Zach, is the winner. Reality Zach’s final three candidates, according to Steve’s confirmation in November 2022, were Kaity Biggar, 27, of Kingston, Ontario; Ariel Frenkel, 28, of New York City; and Gabi, 25, of Pittsford, Vermont.

Ariel was eliminated in the final three, Zach selected Gabi as his winner, and Zach proposed to her at The Bachelor season 27 finale in Krabi, Thailand, according to a November 2022 confirmation from @BachelorWhatever. At the time, @BachelorWhatever posted on Instagram, “I have received enough confirmation from sources and I finally feel confident to share Zach is engaged to Gabriella.”

Bailey Brown Boyfriend

Bailey Brown has dated at least once before she joined the show. Her ex-boyfriend is named Griffin Ganick. They were together since 2017. Their last post together is in July 2019.

Griffin is a native of Brentwood, Tennessee. He is working as a Controller at Rural Health Care Group.

Griffin completed his bachelor’s in accounting from Trevecca Nazarene University and master’s degree in accounting from Haslam College of Business at the University of Tennessee.

How Old Is Bailey Brown?

In January 2023, Bailey Brown is 27 years old. Her date of birth is 26 March 1995.

Bailey Brown Parents

Bailey Brown is the daughter of Hoffman and Jamie Able Brown. Her dad, born in November 1966, is currently 56 years old. Her mom celebrated her 55th birthday in February 2022. There isn’t much about Bailey’s mom, but her dad is an entrepreneur at Brown Sales & Marketing.

Jamie previously worked as VP of Sales at Spin Master Toys. He is a 1990 graduate of Mississippi State University in communications management.

Bailey is one of five children born to her parents. She has two sisters Emerson Brown (@emersonbrown_) and Corrine Brown (@corinnebrown5), and two brothers Parker and Luke Brown.

Emerson works at Brentwood Academy, and studies at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

Corrine was hailed as Athlete of the Week in January 2021 when she was a member of the Varsity Football Cheerleading Team and the Varsity Track & Field Team at Brentwood Academy.

Bailey’s brother Parker Brown studied at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville after graduating from Brentwood Academy. Not much is currently known about Luke.

Bailey Brown Height

Talking about her physical feature, Bailey Brown’s height measures 5’6”.

Bailey Brown Job

Bailey Brown started her career at Magic Time LTD as a business assistant. She worked in the company for only three months. Then she joined Jay Franco and Sons Inc. as a product design & marketing intern for two months.

From 2018 to 2020, she worked first as a marketing intern at ABLE, then as Marketing + Events Coordinator at ABLE. A month after she quit her job at ABLE, she joined BSG Brands as a marketing + product development specialist.

In 2022, Bailey is working at ForceBrands. First, she worked as an executive recruiter then as a senior executive recruiter which is also her current role in the company. According to the company website, “ForceBrands is the leading strategic recruiting firm for consumer brands. Our industry-specific divisions — BevForce, FoodForce, BeautyForce, HerbForce, and PetForce — help employers connect to specialized talent at all levels.”

Bailey enrolled and graduated from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville. She studied communication and earned her Bachelor’s degree in 2018.
