Kylie Jenner desperate to turn swimsuit line around after shes slammed for poor quality

 Posted on September 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  548 words  |  Martina Birk

KYLIE Jenner is “desperate” to turn her swimsuit line around.

The 24-year-old is on a mission after she was slammed for “poor quality fabric and bad stitching.”

Following the launch of her new Kylie Swim collection, the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star was met with backlash as fans slammed the brand’s quality.

Kylie was reportedly surprised by the negative reviews, as a source told Life and Style: “She wasn’t expecting this reaction.

“She’s really freaking out over all the hateful reviews.”

The insider continued: “Kylie used to feel like everything she touched turned to gold, but that just isn’t true anymore, and she’s really upset.”

NEGATIVE REVIEWS

Influencers didn’t hold back as they shared their reviews for the new Kylie Swim collection, as YouTuber Laura Lee complained to her 500,000 TikTok followers: “The whole swimsuit is see-through. I don’t understand. Once I move, everything’s gonna be out. I would never wear this in public.

“They are the most unpractical swimsuits you could buy.”

93 Playstreet swimwear founder Jessica Anderson also called out Kylie for the line’s lack of “integrity,” stating on TikTok: “I am completely and totally disappointed that this is completely see-through.

“I’ve seen behind the scenes of what it takes to produce swimwear, and the fact that no one caught this blows my mind.

“For me, it comes down to lack of integrity as a business owner and an entrepreneur.”

TURNING THINGS AROUND

The pregnant KUWTK star and momager Kris Jenner are taking the criticism into serious consideration, as the source said: “She and Kris are having emergency meetings about what they can do to turn things around.”

“Kylie is concerned enough over the status of each of her current lines,” the insider added.

The informant concluded: “This is supposed to be a time for Kylie to relax, enjoy her pregnancy and plan for the upcoming holidays.

“Instead, she’s questing whether she’s taken on too much — and worrying about the bad vibes affecting her baby.”

MORE BACKLASH

Kylie- who is currently expecting her second child with boyfriend Travis Scott, 30- was recently slammed for her “disturbing” graphic photo shoot.

The couple already share three-year-old daughter Stormi.

In the photos and clips, Kylie was drenched in the fake blood and covered her from head-to-toe in smears.

While wrapping her arms around her body, she gave a serious look to the camera while sitting on a white floor with pools of “blood” gathered around her.

The bizarre photo shoot was to promote Kylie Cosmetics’ Nightmare on Elm Street line.

After Kylie shared the strange promo shots and clips, one critic commented: “This is kind of disturbing especially since you’re pregnant. But that’s just my opinion.

“I’m a mother of two baby girls, so for me it was disturbing seeing this but like I said it’s just my opinion which obviously doesn’t matter.

“I’m a nurse and worked in IVF, saw so many women go through miscarriages etc. The blood looks so real and knowing she’s pregnant makes it weird but yeah just my opinion.”

Another added: “It’s crazy to normalize such a thing especially when you think about women who actually end up like this in tragic events.”
