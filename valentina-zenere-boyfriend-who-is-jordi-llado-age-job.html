Jordi Lladó Family

Jordi Lladó is the grandson of Jose Lladado, former minister of Spain. According to Forbes, Jordi holds a Ph.D. from the Complutense University of Madrid and the University of Madras. Jose founded Tecnicas Reunidas, a construction company he built into a multibillion-dollar business. The success of his company helped him rank 2674 in 2021.

Aside from the business, he served in Spain’s government in the 1970s and as the country’s ambassador to the United States from 1978 to 1982. He is also an art connoisseur, serving as a patron to Spain’s Fundacion de Amigos del Museo del Prado since 1983 and Museo Reina Sofía since 2013.

Jose had five children Pilar Arburua, Pilar, Juan (married to Susana Álvarez Salas), María, José Manuel (married to Marta Tiagonce) and Marta (married to Carlos Romero, son of the Count de Fontao), one of whom is his parents.
