Who is Marshmello?

Chris Comstock who is also known as Marshmello is an American EDM (Electronic Dance Music) producer and DJ.

He is mainly known for his Full head covering white-colored bucket and the song “Alone” which was released in January 2017, ‘Alone’ appeared on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart.

Recently, he released his new song ‘Spotlight’ with rapper Lil Peep.

Marshmello: Early Professional Life, Career

Marshmello’s current profession is still EDM producer and DJ. Recently, he is released ‘Spotlight’ with rapper Lil Peep.

Marshmello first released his original song “WaVeZ” on his SoundCloud page on March 3, 2015. As he was releasing more and more songs he got support from top EDM producers like Skrillex by reposting his song “Find Me” by reposting on his sound cloud page.

2016 was the year Marshmello got successful. He released an album ‘Joytime’ which is consisting of 10 tracks, among those 10 tracks “Keep it Mello” and “Want U 2” were huge successes. On July 2 Mello released another track “Alone” which is one of his greatest hit music of all time. “Alone” got 710 Million views on his main YouTube channel.

In 2017, Marshmello collaborated with famous singers like Selena Gomez and Khalid and released songs like ‘Wolves’ and ‘Silence’ respectively.

Not only this, he has a huge number of fans in his Youtube channel more than 55.50Millions subscribers on his youtube accounts.
