Who is Brian Grazer?

Brian Grazer whose full name is Brian Thomas Grazer was born on July 12, 1951, in Los Angeles, California in the United States of America to his parents, Arlene, a Jewish woman, and criminal defense lawyer Thomas, who was Catholic. He has two younger siblings named Nora and Gavin. When Grazer was in high school, his parents divorced; he was subsequently raised by his little Jewish grandmother. For his higher education, he went to the University of Southern California on a scholarship. Although he began as a psychology major, he ended up graduating from the film school in 1974. Following this, Grazer attended USC Law School for a year but left in 1975 to pursue his career in Hollywood.
