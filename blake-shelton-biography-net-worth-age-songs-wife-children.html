Blake Shelton, born Blake Tollison Shelton, is a famous American television personality and a country music singer. Blake Shelton got into the limelight after the release of his debut single called Austin.

Blake Shelton was born on June 18, 1976, in Ada, Oklahoma, U.S. He was born to Dorothy and Richard Shelton, a beautician and a salesman. Blake Shelton got more popular as a coach on the TV series The Voice. He was born under the birth sign of Gemini and stands at a height of  6 feet 5inches. His siblings are Endy Shelton and Richie Shelton who unfortunately lost his life in an accident.
