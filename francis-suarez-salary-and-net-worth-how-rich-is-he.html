Bio: Francis Suarez Wikipedia

Francis Xavier Suarez was born on October 6, 1977, in Miami. He is the oldest of four children and the son of Rita and Miami mayor Xavier Suarez. Immaculata-LaSalle High School graduated him in 1996.

His bachelor’s degree in finance from Florida International University placed him in the top 10%. He earned a cum laude J.D. from the University of Florida Fredric G. Levin College of Law.
