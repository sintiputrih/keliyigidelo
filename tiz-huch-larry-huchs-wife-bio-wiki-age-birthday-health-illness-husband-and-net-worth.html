Tiz Huch Biography

Tiz Huch is an American co-pastor, television co-host, best-selling author, conference speaker, mentor, as well as entrepreneur. She was born on February 8, 1956, in the United States. Right in the center of the DFW Metroplex – Bedford, Texas, she co-pastors New Beginnings Church, a diverse congregation with her husband Larry.

The church is making an impact, giving people everywhere the hope of a new beginning. She is the co-host of the New Beginnings television show, the weekly broadcast ministry of Larry Huch Ministries. The program’s message is changing lives worldwide as well as covers topics such as breaking generational curses, walking in freedom, enhancing personal as well as family relationships, achieving success, experiencing God’s blessings, and discovering the Jewish roots of the Christian faith.

Author of the best-selling book No Limits, No Boundaries, now in its second printing, she is helping to teach people how to pray for dynamic change in their lives, families, businesses, and futures.

Tiz’s message of tapping into God’s supernatural power every day is striking a positive chord in the lives of thousands everywhere. In 2012, Tiz co-authored Releasing Family Blessings with Larry to empower readers with their insights on God’s plan for families.

Pastors Larry along with Tiz are the proud parents of three wonderful children (and a terrific son-in-law and daughter-in-law) who are all active in ministry. Their 4 grandchildren, the Sugars, are the loves of their lives!
