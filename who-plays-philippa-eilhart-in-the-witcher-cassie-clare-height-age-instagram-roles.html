Who plays Philippa Eilhart in The Witcher? (Cassie Clare height, age, etc.)

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  322 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Philippa Eilhart is an important character in The Witcher universe, featuring in both the book series and the video games. She was officially introduced into the Netflix series during its second season, but Philippa remained in her owl form for most of The Witcher season 2. Actress Cassie Clare only appeared in the season 2 finale. She has a more significant role to play in The Witcher season 3.

Philippa is a powerful sorceress and skilled polymorph working as an advisor to King Vizimir in Redania alongside Sigismund Dijkstra. She’s played by English actress Cassie Clare, a talented performer on both stage and screen. We get to see way more of Clare in the show’s third season, which is now streaming on Netflix.

If you want to learn more about Cassie Clare, keep reading to find out what we know!

Cassie Clare’s age is currently unknown. The actress’s birthday isn’t available online, but we estimate she’s likely in her early 30s.

The statuesque actress is quite tall, standing at 5 feet 9 inches, making her just a few inches shorter than her frequent co-star Graham McTavish, who plays Sigismund Dijkstra.

It looks like @cassie.clare1 is Clare’s official Instagram account. Even though she isn’t yet verified, several cast members follow her there, including her fellow sorceresses Anya Chalotra (Yennefer), Therica Wilson-Read (Sabrina), MyAnna Buring (Tissaia), and Anna Shaffer (Triss).

Clare has worked with Netflix previously in the 2017 sci-fi/action movie What Happened to Monday and in series like The Irregulars and The Sandman (where she played Mazikeen). Some of her other notable roles include Bulletproof 2, Brave New World, Tremors: Shrieker Island, and small parts in the musical films Beauty and the Beast and Mamma Mia! Here We Go Again (which also starred another Witcher star, Hugh Skinner).

Don’t miss seeing Cassie Clare’s scene-stealing performance as Philippa Eilhart in The Witcher season 3, now streaming on Netflix.
