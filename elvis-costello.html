Who is Elvis Costello:

Elvis Costello is a famous Rock Singer. He was born on August 25, 1954 and his birthplace is London, England. Elvis is also well known as, Singer-songwriter who contributed to the proto-new wave and punk movements with such songs as “Pump it Up” and “Allison.” He released his debut album My Aim is True in 1976.

Elvis is originated from England. Born Declan Patrick MacManus, his stage name was inspired by Elvis Presley.
