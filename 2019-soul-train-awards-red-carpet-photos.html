2019 Soul Train Awards Red Carpet Photos Hollywood Life

 Posted on April 23, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  164 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Hosts Tichina Arnold and Tisha Campbell-Martin strike a pose on the red carpet for the 2019 Soul Train Music Awards in Las Vegas, NV on Nov. 17, 2019. The real-life BFF’s were all smiles as they arrived on the teal carpet, and absolutely slayed in their outfits! Tisha rocked a barbie pink pant suot with a low cut black tank and striking silver necklace, while Tichina was giving us serious old Hollywood vibes in a sequin black-and-silver midi-length number with a mesh cutout at the chest. She topped the number with a black overcoat, adding a regal touch.

The two women have been best friends for years, after first meeting on the set of the iconic 1986 film ‘Little Shop of Horrors,’ later appearing as Gina and Sam in the sitcom ‘Martin.’ They made their hosting debut at the BET Soul Train Awards in 2018 and kept the laughs coming, so it’s no surprise to see the ladies back for a second round!
