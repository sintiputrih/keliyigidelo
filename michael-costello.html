Michael Costello was born in Los Angeles, CA on January 20, 1983. Fashion designer who rose to prominence as a finalist on the eighth season of Project Runway. Celebrities have regularly featured his brand on the red carpet, most famously Beyonce at the Grammys in 2014. Gretchen Jones ultimately won Project Runway season 8, where Michael finished fourth. Although his appearance on TV helped propel him, he’d already opened his first store at 15 and went on to intern for Bob Mackie.
