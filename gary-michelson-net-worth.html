Gary Michelson, a prominent inventor and philanthropist, was born in Dazey, North Dakota in 1945. His humble upbringing, without running water or heat in his childhood home, instilled in him a strong work ethic and determination to succeed.

Recognizing the importance of continued learning, he pursued graduate work at North Dakota State University in Fargo.

Maintaining a keen focus on his studies, Michelson laid the foundation for his future achievements.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

One of Gary Michelson’s notable entrepreneurial ventures was the establishment of Tharaldson Companies in 1982.
