Unveiling the Top 5 Most Expensive Hank Aaron Baseball Card Sales

1. 1954 Topps #128 Rookie Card

This masterpiece from the tail-end of the Golden Age of Baseball Trading Cards is an embodiment of historical significance. The orange-backed portrait rookies of Hank Aaron are a testament to his enduring legacy, with the most expensive one fetching an astronomical $645,000. The rarity and pristine condition of this card contribute to its staggering value, making it a true gem for collectors.

 

2. 1963 Topps #380

Jumping ahead two decades into Hank Aaron's illustrious career, the 1963 Topps #380 card captures a candid portrait of the legendary ballplayer during his time with the Milwaukee Braves. This particular card, attaining a gem mint 10 status, holds a remarkable value, selling for a noteworthy $222,000 in 2022 through Heritage Auctions.

 

3. 1968 Topps #110

Continuing our journey through Hank Aaron's career, the 1968 Topps #110 card stands as a testament to his enduring popularity and impact on the sport. With a sale price of $174,000, this card exemplifies the continued resonance of Hank Aaron's legacy in the hearts of baseball enthusiasts and collectors.

 

4. 1960 Topps #300

The 1960 Topps #300 card, capturing a pivotal era in Hank Aaron's career, fetched an impressive $90,000 at auction. This card serves as a tangible link to an era of baseball history that continues to captivate the imagination of collectors worldwide .

5. 1971 Topps #400

Rounding off our list is the 1971 Topps #400 card, which sold for $60,000. This card represents a chapter in Hank Aaron's storied career and remains a cherished item for collectors seeking to own a piece of baseball history.
